    Presenting a new mod that adds an enhancement pack for Yennefer of Vengerberg! This pack includes updated eyes, face, and hair, plus a brand new amulet. For those who prefer to keep Yennefer's face unchanged, a separate file with **just the amulet** is also available.

    I aimed to create my own unique version of Yennefer, drawing inspiration from other mods and the book series. I wanted to give her skin a slightly paler tone. The result is an appearance that I hope you'll enjoy. As for the amulet, I saw its design on Pinterest many years ago and decided to incorporate it into the game.

    <img src="https://i.imgur.com/rabFqWR.png" alt="Yennefer of Vengerberg">

    Please note that this mod **conflicts with the Dynamic Appearances Project** (the original mod is available here: <a href="https://www.nexusmods.com/witcher3/mods/9219">Dynamic Appearances Project</a>). This is because DAP alters outfits and hairstyles for many characters depending on the story's progression. Therefore, I've included a separate file **without my hairstyles**, as DAP already offers many excellent styles of its own. Alternatively, if you're simply not a fan of my hairstyle, you can always download a different hair mod instead.
